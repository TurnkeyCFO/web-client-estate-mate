002 / Our Philosophy

Central Texas roots. Estate-level standards.

Estate Mate was founded in Austin in 2012 by a team that believed Central Texas homeowners deserved better — not just a crew with a mower, but craftsmen who understood the land, the climate, and the plants that thrive here.

We work with property owners across the Hill Country, the Suburbs, and the urban core to maintain, transform, and protect the outdoor spaces that surround the places people call home.

Every plan is custom. Every visit is staffed by our own trained employees — not subcontractors. And we back every job with a satisfaction guarantee.
